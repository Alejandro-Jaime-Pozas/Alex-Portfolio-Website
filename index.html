My Greatest Vacation

Iceland

The pictures above are from my phone! If you ever go to Iceland, I suggest you go at least 5 days and don't take the tourist suggestions. I went there with my family and we made a whole loop of the island in 7 days, staying in different towns every day. Most people make day trips and stay in Reykjavik but I would suggest you take the nomadic route. Summer is the time to visit, you see lush landscapes of all colors, green, orange, purple, black, white ice...There's just a lot to see and everything is outside, it's the ideal nature trip. We had an amazing town and visited places like Reykjavik, Mivatn, Akureyri, Hofn, Vik.
